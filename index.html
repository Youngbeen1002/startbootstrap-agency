Timeline

It was amazing!

  • June 2009

    To Bangkok

    This was my frist oversea trip. It was amazing to see clear seas, elephants, and shows. Howevers, as it was my first trip, I was unable to eat spices so I only ate Dragon fruit for 6 days. But I want to go Thailand again!

  • December 2011

    To Osaka

    This was the trip with my friends. Although it was group tourism, it was invaluable experience. All foods were fantastic and people there were kind. THe most memorable thing is definitely Osaka castle covered with snow.

  • July 2017

    To Danang

    This was my graduation trip to Vietnam. Me and my friends planned together and it was amazing. Although weather was really hot, it was worthy. I love to go to Danang again to enjoy marine sports and their fantastic foods.

  • June to July 2019

    To Europe

    This was my frist time that I go out of Asia. It was my Europe trip to see my friend living in UK. I went England, France, and Spain. As a design major, it was invaluable experience to watch popular arts and design in my eyes. My favorite painting was Van Gogh's Self Portrait.
